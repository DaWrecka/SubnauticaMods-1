[heading][color=#f1c232][b]BULK CRAFTING[/b][/color][/heading][img=right]https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/mods/1155/images/316/316-1600121042-1102966930.jpeg[/img][size=3]Now you can craft multiple items at once when using fabricators. Use [b]mouse wheel[/b] on the [b]crafting tooltip[/b] to change craft amount.
Bulk crafing is [b]compatible with EasyCraft[/b] mod (AutoCraft mode is not supported).

Additional options:[list]
[*][b]Change craft duration[/b] - increase the duration for crafting the items based on the craft amount
[*][b]Change power consumption[/b] - increase the power consumption for crafting the items based on the craft amount (maximum amount for crafting will be limited not only by available ingredients, but also by available power)
[*][b]Faster animations for the items[/b] - ingredients for crafting and crafted items will be fly from/to the inventory faster, based on the craft amount
[/list][/size]
[heading][color=#f1c232][b]PDA TWEAKS[/b][/color][/heading][img=right]https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/mods/1155/images/316/316-1600121054-5075212.jpeg[/img][size=3]Tweaks for PDA includes following  (see screenshots for details):[list]
[*][b]Toggles for beacons[/b] - additional buttons in the Beacon Manager for disabling beacons based on their color (buttons are showing/hiding depending on existing beacons/signals). Disabled beacons will not be controlled by "Toggle All Beacons" button.
[*][b]Allow to clear notifications[/b] - notifications on the PDA tabs can be cleared with right mouse click
[*][b]Tab hotkeys[/b] - switch between the tabs in the PDA with 1-6 keys (or custom keys)
[*][b]Show item count[/b] - show number of items for the each tab on the tab tooltip (number of entries in the databank, number of blueprints etc)
[/list][/size]
[heading][color=#f1c232][b]MISC TWEAKS[/b][/color][/heading][img=right]https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/mods/1155/images/316/316-1600121065-80166579.jpeg[/img][size=3]Various tweaks related to the user interface:[list]
[*][b]Rename beacons in the inventory[/b] - use middle mouse button (or custom hotkey) to rename beacons that are in the inventory (previously was in the [url=https://www.nexusmods.com/subnautica/mods/300]RenameBeacons[/url] mod)
[*][b]Hotkeys for builder menu tabs[/b] - switch between the tabs in the builder menu with 1-5 keys
[*][b]Show save slot ID[/b] - show save slot ID on the load buttons
[*][b]Hide messages while loading[/b] - don't show messages that are added during loading the game
[/list][/size]
